GradFlow – AI companion for CFD engineers.

A compact application for building surrogate models and parameter studies on top of your existing CFD solvers.

Keep your cases and data on your own machines, while using modern machine learning to explore design spaces faster.

Download GradFlow (.zip) Read the quick-start guide

No accounts, no licence server, no always-online requirement – just download, unzip, and start working.

What GradFlow is for

GradFlow is designed to sit beside tools such as OpenFOAM or commercial CFD packages, not to replace them. Use it for:

  • surrogate models trained on CFD data,
  • gradient-aware parameter sweeps,
  • quick design exploration for research and teaching.
